Jason Kuen

Biography

Presently, I am a PhD student under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Wang Gang at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. Prior to this, I was an undergraduate at Multimedia University, with my thesis supervisor being Lim Kian Ming. In brief, my research is about leveraging machine learning algorithms to tackle challenging computer vision problems.
